Services Hospital Peshawar

Services Hospital Peshawar was constructed back in 1854 before independence and remained the only hospital in the provincial capital till the commissioning of Government Lady Reading Hospital. The hospital continued to work as an outpatient Health facility until the year 2000. In the year 2000, an idea to develop the Services Hospital Peshawar originated after going through a bird eye view of the other designated health facilities like Combined Military Hospital for the military personnel, Fauji Foundation Hospital for Ex-service men etc. Then the Provincial Government took a drastic step towards erecting a specialized health facility that would cater for all the medical needs of the provincial civil servants and their families free of cost.This would on one hand facilitate the Government employees by reducing their time wastage at other hospitals during office hours and on the other hand, it would immensely reduce the other hospital’s patient burden.

  • The main concept behind spending so much in Services Hospital Peshawar is to bring it at par with the other Services Hospitals at other provinces.

  • Round the clock availability of specialized and experienced staff along with expert indoor/outdoor facilities at a state of the art building.

  • To Develop advanced Secondary care facilities at Service Hospital Peshawar for provision of effective, timely and appropriate treatment, disease diagnosis, preventive and rehabilitative services to the provincial employees. their families free of cost.
  • To develop a designated hospital for providing continued specialized medical cover to the VIPs & VVIPs of the province including Chief Minister, Governor, Politicians, Judges, Assembly sessions and other VIPs during their session and visits.
